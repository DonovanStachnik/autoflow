How long does setup take?

Most clients are up and running within 15 minutes. We hop on a quick call, I learn about your business, and then I build out your custom reminder system. No technical work required on your end.

What platforms do you integrate with?

We work with most major calendar apps (Google Calendar, Calendly, Square Appointments, Acuity, and more). We can also integrate with your existing CRM or booking software.

Absolutely. You can customize every aspect of the messaging - the timing, the tone, the content. I'll work with you to make sure it matches your brand voice perfectly.
